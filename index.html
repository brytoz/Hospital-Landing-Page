WHO ARE WE

Olidec hospital is driven to provide the best medical care to patients. One of the major goals of Olidec Hospital if to provide affordable health-care and medical services of a high-quality standard to Patients around its environs.

Olidec hospital has been oustanding in its hospitable care since medical services began its operations. So far tertiary services have been introduced to provide adequate care to our patients and some of these include Orthopaedics, internal medicine, Gastroenterology, obstetrics and gynecology, paediatrics, urology, plastic surgery, ENT, opthalmology and cardiology.

At Olidec Hospital, we are committed to serving our patient and bringing a better health-care and clinical service.
