Patients Rights And Responsibilities

At olidec hospital, we ensure that your right to a better health-care is observed
  1. Receive health-care in a safe setting.
  2. Know the names of the names of the person or nurses attending to you.
  3. Patient is expected to obtain a receipt for any payment made.
  4. To be available on appointment and inform the hospital if otherwise.
  5. Right to request for change of ward if for any reason you're uncomfortable with the condition of the current ward.
  6. Right to receive visitors during the time of visitation.
  7. Expect that all records and history pertaining to your care are kept confidential.
  8. Right to know their diagnosis and prognosis unless otherwise.
